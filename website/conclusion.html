# What have we done

Conclusion

During adolescence, teens develop emotionally, cognitively, and physically. The life of a teenager seems to change daily. One minute a teen seems interested in a new sport, topic in school, or type of music, relationships, etc. In this project, we have explored the life of teens. Teens want more independence and more emotional distance between them and their family. Therefore, they always have complicated emotions like trust, anticipation, fear, joy, sadness towards family members. According to our emotional analysis, teen’s positive emotions is still more than negative emotions.


A teen’s focus often shifts to social interactions and friendships. This includes same-gender groups of friends and cross-gender groups of friends. That’s why some of them are enthusiastic about sharing the experience of first kiss and some of them are enthusiastic about sharing their same gender loving. The topic of gay group is very popular on reddit. In our research, we found that the average first kiss age is 13.5 while most teens got their first kiss at 15. Sadly, youth not only goes with love and happiness, so we also do research about teenagers’ bullying situation. The bullying rate is significantly higher in the lower grades than in the higher grades and girls are more likely to have the experience of being bullied than boys. Bully is a serious problem and deserves a deep research, so we focus a lot on that and changed one of our initial proposals to that.

Besides of relationships, hobbies and education for teenagers can also sometimes be life-defining moments. To understand the schooling of American youth, we used an external dataset “High schools’ graduation rate” and found that the sample number from school year 2020-2021 experience a great reduce which may be due to the influence of Covid-19. California and Texas have the highest accumulated number of students who graduated, indicating these two states have richer educational resources. Teens’ hobbies can be various. We built Association Rule Mining model via Apriori Algorithm to find the teens’ favorite things. Then we chose three popular hobbies: music, art and sport to build a classification model. The accuracy of this classification is quite satisfying, so it is useful for Reddit to manage pricing and slot of relevant ads. It is a pity that we cannot figure out the initial question 7 regarding games we proposed. It is hard to distinguish the name of games they mentioned as teenagers love to use abbreviation or typical characters instead of the full name of a game.

To help Reddit have a better advertisement management system, we conducted text analysis on the number of comments overtime, selected top 20 common words in teenager community and plotted the distribution of comments length. Teen’s most active time is from 1am to 4am, which means the price of ads during this time can be higher. From the list of common words, we found that some pet phrases are very popular among teens, which indicates that words in the ads can use these words to attract teens’ attention. From the length distribution of comments, we can know that teens are used to communicate with the shortest words, so the ads shouldn’t be too long, or teens have no patient to read. The number of comments is the most important factor in judging an author's performance, so the average number of comments an author submits can be used as an indicator for advertisers to measure whether an author is worthy of cooperation.
